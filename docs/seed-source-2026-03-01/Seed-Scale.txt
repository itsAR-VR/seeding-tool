1.0 Executive Overview
Project Seed-Scale is an end-to-end workflow automation designed to manage high-volume influencer seeding. Its purpose is to drastically reduce manual coordinator hours, eliminate human error in order processing, and create a scalable, repeatable system for generating organic content.
This v1.0 build is fully operational and ready for initial testing. It transforms the seeding process from a series of manual tasks into a centralized, automated flow, allowing one coordinator to manage the workload of several.
2.0 The Automated Workflow (Step-by-Step)
The system operates on a trigger-and-action basis, managed from a central Google Sheet.
1. [MANUAL] Data Input: The process begins when an outreach coordinator populates a new row in the Google Sheet with an identified influencer's essential data (Name, Instagram Handle, Email).
2. Automated Email Outreach: Upon QA confirmation, triggers a pre-approved, brand-specific email sequence from the brand's designated email alias.
3. AI-Powered Response Handling: The integrated AI agent monitors the email thread for replies. It is trained to understand responses and engage in a natural conversation to acquire the necessary shipping information (Full Address, Product Choice, Size, Color, etc.).
4. Automated Draft Order Creation: Once the AI successfully obtains the address, it connects to the brand's Shopify store via API and upon APPROVAL within the sheet creates a complete draft order with the correct products and Order Info [Tracking Number, Order ID, Fulfilment Status] being posted within the sheet.
5. [MANUAL] Fulfillment: The brand's fulfillment team sees the new draft order and fulfills it as normal. 
6. Delivery Confirmation: The system continuously monitors the tracking number to confirm the package's delivery status.
7. Automated Post-Delivery Follow-Up: 24 hours after a confirmed delivery, the system sends a pre-scripted follow-up email (e.g., "Hope you love the product! Can't wait to see what you think."). [To finalize by 11-06-25]
8. Content Tracking (via Refunnel): The system connects to Refunnel to monitor the influencer's handle for new posts that tag the brand. It pulls the link for the content itself and the date for when it was posted. [Still to do is multiple posts with their relevant links being pulled in separate columns]
9. Automated Non-Post Follow Up: If the influencer hasn't posted another 2 days after the first message at that point triggers another message, which then follows up with them again. 


10. Manual Intervention Flag: If an influencer does not respond to the initial email sequence after a set period or asks a question that requires human intervention, the corresponding cell in the Google Sheet is flagged RED, signaling to the coordinator that a manual intervention (e.g., an Instagram DM/email) is required. Once the coordinator manually obtains the info and inputs it, the automation resumes from Step 4.
3.0 Per-Brand Deployment Requirements
To deploy Project Seed-Scale for a new client, the following assets are required:
* Shopify API & Webhook : A private app must be created with these permissions. [write_customers, read_customers, write_draft_orders, read_draft_orders, read_inventory, write_order_edits, read_order_edits, write_orders, read_orders, read_products, read_shipping, read_third_party_fulfillment_orders, read_custom_fulfillment_services, write_custom_fulfillment_services, read_fulfillment_constraint_rules, write_fulfillment_constraint_rules] 

Webhooks to our n8n workflow needs to be created in Shopify with a link to the production environment which is brand dependent. 
[example: https://companyname.app.n8n.cloud/webhook/shopify/fulfillment-created • JSON]

* Dedicated Email Account: A G-Suite email account or alias for the brand (e.g., collabs@brand.com) to be used for all automated outreach.
* Brand Persona & Voice Prompt: A 30-minute interview or a detailed document outlining the brand's tone of voice, key value propositions, and rules of engagement for the AI response agent.
* Email Sequence Copy: Pre-approved copy for the initial outreach and post-delivery follow-up emails.
* N8N account: All brand automations can be run through the same n8n account, but one needs to be provided for all workflows and automations to be in one place. 








4.0 v1.0 Limitations & v2.0 Feature Roadmap
This initial version is a robust proof of concept with clear areas for expansion.
   * Current v1.0 Limitations:
   * Does not automate the initial influencer identification process. This remains a manual task.
   * Does not automate or read Instagram DMs;.
   * Content tracking is limited to [Platform, Handle, Follower Count, Media URL, Media Type, Date Posted]
   * Proposed v2.0 Roadmap (incorporating our discussion):
   * Automated Usage Rights: Upon detecting a post, trigger a secondary AI agent to automatically request usage rights via email or DM. 
   * Advanced Content Ingestion: Integrate a tool to automatically download new content (video and static) once usage rights are secured.
   * AI-Powered Creative Editing: Connect the ingestion system to an AI video editor (e.g., based on the Sora/Imagen models) to automatically reformat content into ad-ready assets (9x16, 1x1, 4x5) and add brand-approved captions/headlines.
   * Automated Asset Delivery: Push the final, ad-ready assets directly into the brand's designated Dropbox/Google Drive folder and, ultimately, into the ad account.
   * Advanced Influencer Identification: Develop/Connect to an AI scraper to proactively identify on-brand influencers based on a complex set of criteria (persona, audience demographics, content style, engagement patterns), creating a continuously refreshed target list.
